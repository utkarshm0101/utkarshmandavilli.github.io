About Me

I am a PCB design engineer with expertise in hardware design, signal integrity, and power delivery analysis. Proficient in Altium Designer, I have hands-on experience designing custom PCBs, analyzing circuit behavior, and optimizing layouts for performance. My work spans from fundamental circuit analysis to advanced board design, including high-speed digital circuits and microcontroller-based systems.

Lab 5: Voltage Droop & Decoupling Capacitors

Built a slammer circuit to create switching noise and induce voltage droop on power rails. Studied the effectiveness of decoupling capacitors in mitigating these effects and analyzed the impact of capacitor placement relative to components and power rails.

Investigation of how aggressor signals create noise in victim signal return paths. Demonstrated the critical importance of maintaining continuous return planes for signal integrity in high-speed designs.
